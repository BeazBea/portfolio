The Best Movies According to Bea

My Top 3 movies of all-time

  1. YES

    2. Director: Sally Potter

    She is the bored wife of British politician Anthony. At a dinner party, she meets him, a Lebanese exile who, a professional in his own land, works in London as a cook.

  2. ACRIMONY

    3. Director: Tyler Perry

    When Melinda met Robert, she was sceptical of his charming nature, but couldn't deny how attracted she was to him. Once they fell in love, Melinda moulded herself into everything he wanted her to be.

  3. THE SIX TRIPLE EIGHT

    4. Director: Tyler Perry

    Eight hundred and fifty-five join the war effort to address a three-year mail backlog. Despite discrimination and war-torn conditions, they sort over seventeen million pieces of mail ahead of schedule
